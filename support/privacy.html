Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Overview

The Social Theory Quiz app is designed to respect your privacy. It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

  • No user accounts
  • No analytics
  • No tracking
  • No data sent to servers — the app runs entirely on your device

On-Device Data

If the app stores your quiz progress or settings, this information stays on your device and is not shared or uploaded.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party SDKs for advertising, analytics, or tracking.
